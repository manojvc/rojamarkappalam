Roja Mark Appalam

We are a manufacturer of organic papad, chips appalam and ring appalam, established in Madurai, Tamil Nadu. Roja Mark Appalam is made of pure orid dhal flour and prepared handmade. To make it tastier, lemon, coconut oil and asafoetida are used.

We provide best quality, best price and packaging in different weights and sizes as per customer requirement. We are proud to say that using handmade pappad also creates good job opportunities for families.

Our story

Shri M.V. Antony and family established the business on 08-09-1975 in Madurai. With decades of experience in the pappad industry, Roja Mark Appalam has become a trusted brand in Madurai.

We accept marriage and bulk order distribution and dealership enquiries.
